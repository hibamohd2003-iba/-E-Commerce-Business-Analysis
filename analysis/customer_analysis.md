## 🚨 Critical Issues Identified

### Issue 1: Zero Repeat Purchase Culture
- **Severity:** CRITICAL
- **Impact:** R$11.2M at risk
- **Root Cause:** No retention strategy, no post-purchase engagement

### Issue 2: Satisfaction Variability
- **Severity:** HIGH
- **Impact:** Customers not returning
- **Root Cause:** Quality issues with some sellers

### Issue 3: Declining Cohort CLV
- **Severity:** HIGH
- **Impact:** New customers worth less than old ones
- **Root Cause:** Market saturation or deteriorating value

## 💡 Recommendations

### Immediate Actions (Month 1-3)
1. **Launch Loyalty Program** - 20% repeat rate target
2. **Post-Purchase Email Sequence** - Days 1, 3, 7, 14, 30
3. **Win-Back Campaign** - Target 18,636 dormant customers

### Medium-Term (Month 4-9)
1. **Quality Improvement Program** - Address seller issues
2. **Referral Program** - 5% of customers acquire new ones
3. **Personalization Engine** - Recommendation system

### Long-Term (Month 10-12)
1. **Subscription Model** - Monthly subscription option
2. **VIP Program** - Exclusive benefits for top 25%
