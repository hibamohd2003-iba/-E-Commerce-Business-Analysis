The Brazilian e-commerce platform generated **R$15.84M in total revenue** across **99,441 orders** from September 2016 to October 2018, demonstrating strong market demand but with concerning growth deceleration trends.

## Financial Health

### Margin Analysis
- **Apr 2017:** 85.42% (peak)
- **Jul 2018:** 81.77% (current)
- **Decline:** 3.65 percentage points
- **Annual Impact:** R$496K-547K erosion

**Root Cause:** Shipping costs growing faster than revenue

---

## Insights & Recommendations

### ✅ Strengths
1. **High delivery success rate (97%)**
2. **Strong absolute revenue (R$15.8M)**
3. **Low cancellation rate (0.63%)**
4. **Consistent order values across days**

### ⚠️ Challenges
1. **Geographic concentration** (42% from 1 state)
2. **Margin erosion** (85.4% → 81.8%)
3. **Plateau in growth** (declining momentum)
4. **Lost orders** (R$218K stuck)

### 🎯 Recommendations
1. **Expand to Northeast** - Target underserved regions
2. **Optimize logistics** - Address margin erosion
3. **Recover lost orders** - Process stuck invoices
4. **Leverage seasonality** - Plan inventory for peaks
