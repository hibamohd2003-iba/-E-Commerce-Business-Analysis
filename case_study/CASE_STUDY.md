# E-Commerce End-to-End Business Transformation Analysis
## Case Study

**Dataset:** Brazilian E-Commerce (Olist) — Kaggle  
**Scope:** 99,441 orders | Sep 2016 – Oct 2018 | 9 tables  
**Tools:** SQLite, SQL (50+ queries), Python (Pandas), PowerPoint  

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

A Brazilian e-commerce platform had strong acquisition numbers but was losing money through poor retention, regional concentration, and operational inefficiencies. The goal was to identify root causes and build a data-driven transformation roadmap.

---

## 🔍 What I Did

1. **Explored** 99,441 orders across 9 tables using SQL
2. **Identified** 6 critical business problems with quantified impact
3. **Built** financial models showing 3-year transformation potential
4. **Wrote** a 15-page Business Requirements Document
5. **Created** a 4-phase implementation roadmap with ROI analysis

---

## 📊 Key Findings

### Problem 1: Retention Crisis (CRITICAL)
- **100% single-purchase rate** — zero repeat customers
- 41,118 customers at risk holding R$6.4M in value
- Returning customers spend **65% more** (R$249 vs R$150)

### Problem 2: Regional Concentration
- SP alone = 42% of all customers
- Top 3 states = 63% of total revenue
- Northern states completely untapped

### Problem 3: Product Quality
- Office Furniture rated 3.49/5 (1,687 reviews, 440 unhappy customers)
- PC Gamer: 33% dissatisfaction rate
- Men's & Women's Fashion both in bottom 10

### Problem 4: Operational Delays
- 12.1 day average fulfillment (target: under 10)
- AL (Alagoas): only 76% on-time delivery
- 7,826 late deliveries total

### Problem 5: Margin Erosion
- Margins declining 85.4% → 81.8% over 2 years
- R$547K in lost profit from margin erosion alone
- Shipping costs growing faster than revenue

### Problem 6: Payment Bottlenecks
- R$218K stuck in canceled/unavailable/invoiced orders
- 314 orders stuck in invoiced status
- 609 unavailable orders losing R$85K
